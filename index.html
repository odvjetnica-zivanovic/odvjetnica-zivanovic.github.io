About


Attorney at law Renata Živanović was born in Zagreb where she finished her primary education and her secondary education at Centar za upravu i pravosuđe u Zagrebu, smjer upravni referent. She graduated at Pravni fakultet u Zagrebu in 1988.

Following her apprenticeship in a renowned law firm in Zagreb in the period between 1988 and 1992 and a passed bar exam issued by the contemporary Ministarstvo pravosuđa RH, Renata Živanović was enlisted into Imenik odvjetnika pri Hrvatskoj odvjetničkoj komori with the start of 1993 and has been working as an attorney at law since then.

According to valid regulations, the Attorneys' Code of Ethics and the limits of attorney's field of expertise, attorney at law Renata Živanović offers all forms of legal help, especially legal advice, document drafting and representation before the court, administrative entities and any other entities or organisations.

Real estate attorney Renata Živanović offers services and legal help in all branches of civil law, as well as all infringement and administrative proceedings.
